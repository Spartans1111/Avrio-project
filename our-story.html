AVRIO is a Greek word meaning "tomorrow" or "the future."  Established in the year 2022, At AVRIO we carry a sense of anticipation and forward-looking optimism, embodying the promise of what is yet to come.



"Avrio" often reflects hope and the potential for new beginnings, signifying a belief in positive change and progress. Thus capturing the essence of looking ahead with confidence and eagerness.



We focus on Improving the knowledge, understanding and practical skills of the Officers involved in the handling and management of LNG on ships and terminals, using our specially designed simulator. Raising the level of your training and safety onboard with our hyper realistic cloud based LNG Training simulators.
